
## Above and Beyond Features

1. Full Keyboard support with the "Enter" key being able to guess and restart games, and the "D" key being able to switch difficulties.
2. Styles to make the website more readable and polished
3. Dark mode toggle with an animation that can prepare you for the "flash" indicated by light mode. The animation gives the user time to look away from their screen if they think the sudden increase in brightness will damage their eyes, or if it was a missclick.
